About Entreprinder

Entreprinder is the premier platform connecting entrepreneurs in Luxembourg.

Our Mission

At Entreprinder, we believe in the power of connection and collaboration. Our mission is to foster a vibrant entrepreneurial ecosystem in Luxembourg by providing a platform where innovators, creators, and business leaders can come together, share ideas, and build successful ventures.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Entreprinder was born out of a need for a centralized hub where Luxembourg's diverse and dynamic entrepreneurial community could thrive. We recognized the unique challenges and opportunities that Luxembourg's business landscape presents, and created a tailored solution to address these needs.

What We Offer

  • A user-friendly platform for networking and collaboration
  • Access to local events and workshops
  • Resources tailored to Luxembourg's business environment
  • Opportunities for mentorship and knowledge sharing

Join us in shaping the future of entrepreneurship in Luxembourg!
